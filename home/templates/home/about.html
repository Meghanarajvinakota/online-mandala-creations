About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of artists , after decades of teaching had a thought: "Just because we're teaching mandala arts, doesn't mean our teaching itself has to be old-fashioned!" We created online mandala creations to embody that idea; to use all of the knowledge that exists today in order to modernize the way we teach the mandala art.
